Who Are We?

Trek & Tread Africa is an adventure-driven company dedicated to creating unforgettable biking, hiking, and team-building experiences across Africa’s most breathtaking landscapes. We blend exploration, wellness, and human connection to help individuals and teams reconnect with nature and each other.
